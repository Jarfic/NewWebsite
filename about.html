About Us

Mission statement: At Rstick One our goal is to make not just functioning controllers, but controllers made with quality and comfort across all gaming platforms for a price within reason.

Our Purpose

Throughout the years i've used a fair amount of controllers. And while i may admit part of the reason is myself, often, over extended use they begin to wear down and not function properly. When buying new controllers the range is skewed from getting a cheap controller that break down even faster or getting an overly expensive controller that doesn't need to be. I've seen others have the problem of not being able to get more controllers and/or having completely busted ones because of this. This business's purpose is to be that halfway point.
